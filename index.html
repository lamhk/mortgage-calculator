What is an offset mortgage?

Money in an offset account reduces the balance you pay interest on (it usually does not reduce your loan balance). Your repayment can look the same, but interest drops and you may finish sooner. Show explanation ↓

Offset mortgage: how this calculator treats it
  • Effective principal = max(0, remaining balance − offset balance)
  • Interest each period is calculated on the effective principal
  • Your payment amount stays the same for a standard repayment mortgage — the offset mainly changes the interest/principal split
  • This calculator assumes the offset balance is constant (no deposits/withdrawals over time)
