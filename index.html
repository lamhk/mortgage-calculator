Mortgage Calculator

Monthly mortgage + (optional) shared ownership rent. Shows interest/capital split at the 13th and 25th payments.

Inputs
Shared ownership
If enabled, mortgage is calculated on the owned share; rent is added for the unowned share.
This asks for the annual rent rate charged on the unowned share. Default market assumption is 2.7%.
Note: For repayment mortgages, the interest/capital split changes every month. The snapshots below show the splits at the 13th and 25th mortgage payments.
