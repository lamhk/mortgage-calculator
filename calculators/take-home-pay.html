How this calculator works
  • Personal Allowance: £12,570 (tapers down above £100,000).
  • Income tax bands: 20% / 40% / 45% on taxable income (England/Wales/NI model).
  • Employee NI (Category A): 0% up to threshold, then 8%, then 2% above upper limit.
  • Student loan: a % of income above your plan threshold (simplified annual calculation).
